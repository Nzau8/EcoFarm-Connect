Frequently Asked Questions

Click on "Sign Up", select "Seller" role, and fill in your details including farm location and contact information. Once registered, you can start listing your organic products.

You can sell organic farm produce, including fruits, vegetables, grains, and other agricultural products that meet our quality standards.

We exclusively use M-Pesa for all transactions. When making a purchase, simply enter your M-Pesa number at checkout and follow the prompts on your phone to complete the secure payment.

Once registered, you can access the Community section to join discussions, share experiences, ask questions, and connect with other farmers and buyers interested in sustainable agriculture.

We partner with local boda riders who handle deliveries. Once an order is placed, it's assigned to a nearby rider who picks up the products from the seller and delivers them to the buyer.

The Learning Hub is available to all registered users. Access educational content, video tutorials, and expert webinars about sustainable farming practices through the Learning Hub section.

We accept various payment methods including M-Pesa, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and protected.
